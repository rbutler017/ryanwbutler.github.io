Welcome to the personal webpage of Ryan W. Butler

Hello! I'm Ryan Butler, a first year MS student in RIT's astrophysical sciences and technology program.

I did my undergraduate degree in physics and mathematics at Alfred University. While their I spent a lot of time at Stull Observatory observing (or attemempting to observe) exoplanet transits.

I currently work on nearby young stars with Dr. Joel Kastner. These are stars within 100 pc of the sun and of ages less than 50 or so Myr. At present I am working on producing X-ray SEDs of several of candidate stars observed by Chandra as part of the NYSCATS program.
